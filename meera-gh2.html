Where Meera GH2 creates value

An integrated offering across the hydrogen lifecycle keeps innovators, investors, and regulators aligned.

Electrolyser labs

Design and deployment of modular alkaline and PEM electrolyser skids (5–250 Nm³/hr) powered through rooftop and ground-mount solar.

Fuel cell integration

Turnkey integration of PEM fuel cells for backup and mobility pilots, including data logging and stack health analytics.

EPC & safety consultancy

Comprehensive engineering support – from hazard analysis to piping & instrumentation diagrams and O&M training.

Capability building

STEM-aligned curriculum kits, hackathons, and internship cohorts enabling students to co-create hydrogen solutions.

Roadmap to 2027

Milestones guiding the Meera GH2 initiative as India's hydrogen ecosystem matures.

  • 2025 · Pilot labs & curriculum

    Commission hydrogen learning labs in Jharkhand universities with working electrolyser & fuel cell rigs.

  • 2026 · Industrial proof-of-concept

    Deploy 500 Nm³/day production with solar + battery hybridisation for captive industrial clients.

  • 2027 · Mobility & refuelling

    Build green hydrogen dispensing micro-hubs for fleets and municipal buses alongside long-term AMC services.
