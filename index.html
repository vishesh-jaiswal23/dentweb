Frequently asked questions

Clear answers on subsidies, compliance, and metering keep your project moving smoothly.

How does the PM Surya Ghar subsidy process work with Dakshayani?

Our subsidy cell raises your application on the PM Surya Ghar portal within 24 hours of signing. We coordinate site photographs, DISCOM feasibility, and documentation uploads. Once inspection is complete, the subsidy is credited directly to your bank account while we reconcile the balance with transparent invoices.

  • Dedicated case manager for every household or MSME
  • Weekly status updates via WhatsApp and email
  • Support with Aadhaar e-sign, net-metering forms, and bank coordination
What is the typical timeline for net-metering approvals in Jharkhand?

For LT rooftop systems, approvals arrive within 21 working days on average. HT and commercial connections may take 30–45 days depending on load sanction. Our engineering desk submits drawings, follows up with the DISCOM office, and schedules meter installation so you can start exporting power without delays.

  • Pre-filled DISCOM forms and load details submitted by our team
  • Joint inspection with DISCOM engineers for faster closure
  • Commissioning report shared with you and stored in our customer portal
