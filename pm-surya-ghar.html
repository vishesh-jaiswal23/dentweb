The scheme at a glance

The Government of India covers a significant portion of your rooftop solar cost, ensuring 300 free units every month for eligible homeowners.

  • Subsidy of ₹30,000, ₹60,000 and ₹78,000 for 1, 2 and 3 kW+ systems respectively.
  • Direct benefit transfer into the beneficiary's bank account after commissioning.
  • Optional low-interest financing support from partner banks.
  • 25-year module warranty & 5-year inverter assurance.

How we navigate the portal for you

Every stage is handled in-house – you simply share documents and sign digitally.

  • Application & feasibility

    Our team registers you on the National Portal, uploads KYC, and coordinates the DISCOM feasibility report.

  • Design, sanction & installation

    We conduct site survey, finalise structural drawings, secure approvals, and commission the plant with net meter wiring.

  • Inspection & subsidy disbursement

    Post-installation, we host DISCOM inspection, upload completion reports, and track the DBT credit to your bank.

Eligibility & documentation checklist

We provide templates and assist with notarisation or DISCOM-specific formats.

Documents required

  • Recent electricity bill (JBVNL / local DISCOM)
  • Aadhaar card & PAN of applicant
  • Cancelled cheque for subsidy credit
  • Property ownership proof / NOC

Eligibility criteria

  • Residential consumer category (LT connection)
  • No prior subsidy availed on the premise
  • Adequate roof space & structural integrity
  • Net-metering agreement with DISCOM

How long does subsidy disbursement take?

Once the net meter is commissioned and the completion report is accepted, DBT credits are typically released within 30–45 working days. Our admin dashboard keeps you informed at every stage.

Can tenants or housing societies apply?

Yes. We help structure society resolutions and NOCs so cooperative housing projects and renters with landlord consent can participate.

What if my roof needs civil work?

We conduct a structural audit and can integrate lightweight elevated structures or waterproofing before installation. Associated costs can be bundled into financing.
