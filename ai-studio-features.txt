Here is a list of features for the new AI Studio page:

*   **Content Generation:**
    *   Generate blog posts from a topic or keywords.
    *   Generate social media posts (e.g., Twitter, LinkedIn) from a topic or URL.
    *   Generate email marketing copy (e.g., newsletters, promotional emails).
*   **Image Generation:**
    *   Generate images from a text description.
    *   Create variations of an existing image.
    *   Generate images in different styles (e.g., photorealistic, cartoon, abstract).
*   **Text-to-Speech:**
    *   Convert text to audio in different voices and languages.
    *   Generate audio for videos, podcasts, or presentations.
*   **AI Chatbot:**
    *   A chatbot for answering customer questions.
    *   A chatbot for internal support.
*   **Settings and Configuration:**
    *   Configure API keys for different AI services.
    *   Set usage limits and monitor costs.
    *   Customize the behavior of the AI models.
